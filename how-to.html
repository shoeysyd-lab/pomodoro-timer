How to Use the Pomodoro Timer

Getting Started

Welcome to your productivity companion! This guide will walk you through using the Pomodoro Timer effectively to boost your focus and productivity.

Step 1: Choose Your Work Mode

Select from the available timer modes:

  • Work (25 min): Standard Pomodoro work session
  • Short Break (5 min): Quick break between work sessions
  • Long Break (15 min): Extended break after 4 work sessions
  • Custom: Set your own timer duration

Using the Timer

Step 2: Start Your Session

Click the "Start" button to begin your timer. The timer will count down from your selected duration.

Pro Tip: Choose a task to focus on before starting the timer.

Step 3: Work Focused

During your work session:

  • Put away distractions (phone, social media)
  • Focus solely on your chosen task
  • If a distraction arises, note it down and return to work
  • Use the hourglass animation as a visual reminder

Step 4: Take Breaks

When the timer reaches zero:

  • You'll hear an audio notification
  • Take a complete break from your work
  • Stand up, stretch, or take a short walk
  • Avoid checking work-related emails or messages

Advanced Usage

Quick Timer Options

For shorter tasks or when you need flexibility:

  • 1 Minute: Perfect for quick tasks or testing
  • 3 Minutes: Good for short focused bursts
  • 5 Minutes: Standard short break duration

Click any of these buttons to start a quick timer instantly.

Session Tracking

The app tracks your completed sessions:

  • View your daily progress
  • See how many Pomodoros you've completed
  • Track your productivity patterns

Custom Timers

For specialized needs:

  • Set any duration from 1 second to 99 minutes
  • Perfect for different types of tasks
  • Ideal for creative work or complex problems

Best Practices

Daily Routine

  • Start your day with a clear task list
  • Group similar tasks together
  • Plan your breaks in advance
  • Review your progress at the end of the day

During Work Sessions

  • Work on one task at a time
  • Silence notifications
  • Keep your workspace clean and organized
  • Stay hydrated and comfortable

Break Optimization

  • Use breaks for physical movement
  • Practice deep breathing or meditation
  • Avoid screens during short breaks
  • Prepare for the next work session

Troubleshooting

Timer not working?

Make sure your browser allows notifications for audio alerts. Try refreshing the page if issues persist.

Can't focus during sessions?

Start with shorter sessions and gradually increase. Ensure your environment is conducive to focus.

Forgetting to take breaks?

The audio notifications will remind you. Consider setting phone reminders as backup.
